Lights Out — Logo System

Brand mark for the Windows bedtime shutdown utility. Dark lamp, digital alarm-clock glow, countdown ring.

Articulated desk lamp + "LIGHTS OUT" in segmented digital alarm-clock style. OUT carries a gentle left-to-right fade; O is a partial countdown ring.

5. Design Notes

  • Lamp: Sharp geometric articulated desk lamp built from mitered paths. No rounding, no soft curves. Base → two arm segments → trapezoid shade pointing right.
  • Light beam: Semi-transparent cone from shade opening across the text area. Gradient fades from #00FFD1 at source to transparent at right edge.
  • "LIGHTS": Custom blocky/geometric letterforms in #00FFD1. Designed to evoke digital alarm-clock segment displays without being literal 7-segment.
  • "OUT": Intentional fade. O is a partial glowing countdown ring (stroke-dasharray). U is dim (~45% opacity). T is mostly dark (~18% opacity, just recognizable).
  • Countdown ring: The broken circle with flickering dash gaps is the core brand motif. It appears in the wordmark O, the app icon, and the tray icon.
  • Small-size strategy: Tray and small app icons drop the wordmark entirely. They reduce to: lamp shade silhouette + bold broken ring + minimal flicker dots. Stroke widths are intentionally thick (4-5px in 64px viewBox) so they survive 16px rasterization.
  • No copyrighted characters. No Pixar/Luxo likeness. The lamp is a generic articulated architectural desk lamp with all-miter joints and sharp trapezoid shade.
  • No yellow light. All glow is teal/cyan (#00FFD1 family).
  • No tagline text. Only "LIGHTS OUT" appears in the full wordmark.
